Xiaomi's Story

Xiaomi was founded in 2010 by Lei Jun and others. Starting from its initial mobile business, Xiaomi gradually expanded into multiple fields such as smart homes and artificial intelligence, committed to enabling everyone to enjoy the fun of technology.

Our mission

Xiaomi's mission is to always adhere to making good products that touch people's hearts and are reasonably priced, so that everyone in the world can enjoy high-quality technology products from China.

Our values

  • Sincere love: Sincerely love users and think from their perspective.
  • Teamwork: mutual trust, mutual support, and common growth.
  • Embrace change: dare to innovate, dare to try, and flexibly respond to challenges.
  • Pursuing excellence: Pursuing excellent quality and constantly breaking through oneself.

Our team

Xiaomi has a diverse team consisting of top engineers, designers, and marketing experts. They have rich professional knowledge and practical experience in hardware development, software design, user experience, and marketing

Our team has always maintained a passion for technology and a pursuit of innovation, committed to providing users with the best products and services.
